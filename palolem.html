Palolem Beach

Wave Height: 1.2 meters
Wind Speed: 10 km/h
Water Activities: Kayaking, Paddleboarding, Dolphin Spotting
Specialties: Crescent-shaped Beach, Calm Waters, Picturesque Sunset
More Info: Palolem Beach is known for its serene and tranquil environment. It is a popular destination for backpackers and those looking for a peaceful retreat. The beach is lined with palm trees and offers stunning views of the setting sun.
